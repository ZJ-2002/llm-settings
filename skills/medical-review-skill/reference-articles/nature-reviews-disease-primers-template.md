## 写作风格特点

### 语言特征
1. **客观性**：使用"evidence suggests"、"studies demonstrate"
2. **准确性**：具体数据、置信区间、P值
3. **平衡性**：呈现争议，不偏袒
4. **简洁性**：每句话有明确信息量

### 典型句式
```
- "XX is a [condition] affecting [population]..."
- "Evidence from [study type] demonstrates that..."
- "However, [contradictory evidence] suggests..."
- "Current guidelines recommend..."
- "The mechanism underlying XX remains to be elucidated."
- "Future research should focus on..."
```

### 数据呈现
- 患病率：百分比+绝对数（7.6%, equating to approximately 595 million individuals）
- 效应量：OR/RR + 95%CI（OR of 1.98, 95% CI 1.57–2.20）
- 时间趋势：从XX到XX（from 4.8% in 1990 to 7.6% in 2020）
